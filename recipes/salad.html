Pesto Caprese Salad

Description

Fresh cherry or grape tomatoes, vibrant pesto sauce, creamy fresh mozzarella balls, extra-virgin olive oil, balsamic glaze, basil, and spices. A perfect summer salad recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pint Grape or Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 8 ounces Fresh Mozzarella Balls
  • 2 Tablespoons Pesto Sauce
  • 2 to 3 Tablespoons Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Glaze (drizzle on)
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Cut grapes in half. In a large bowl, add tomatoes. Sprinkle with salt. Drain fresh mozzarella and toss fresh mozzarella balls, pesto sauce, olive oil, balsamic glaze, and fresh basil into the bowl. Taste for seasoning.
